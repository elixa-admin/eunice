# Eunice School Brand Knowledgebase

Last updated: 2026-05-24
Primary source: [euniceps.co.za](https://euniceps.co.za/)
Secondary source checked: [Eunice Girls High School on LinkedIn](https://nl.linkedin.com/school/eunice-girls-high-school/)

This document captures the school identity, tone, staff structure, and cultural cues that should inform Eunice admissions UI, copy, and dashboard presentation.

## 1. School Identity

- Official family of pages observed on the primary website: Eunice Primary & Pre-Primary.
- The school presents itself as a long-established girls’ school community rooted in Bloemfontein.
- Founded in 1875 as the Oranje Vrij Staat Dames Instituut, on the initiative of the Dutch Reformed Church.
- The name “Eunice” is explained on the site as a biblical reference to the mother of Timothy and a Greek phrase meaning “happy victory”.
- The school’s long-form brand statement emphasizes tradition, standards, values, academic excellence, quality teaching, social responsibility, and all-round development.
- The motto shown on the site is “Vincit qui se vincit” and the founding ideal cites Psalm 144:12.

## 2. Location And Positioning

- Primary and pre-primary contact pages list the address as 3 Jock Meiring Street, Park West, Bloemfontein.
- The about page says the school is in spacious surroundings bordering Grey College and the University of the Free State.
- The site language places the school in a proud, historic Bloemfontein girls’ school tradition.
- The website also exposes a linked High School pathway, which suggests the wider Eunice ecosystem includes multiple school stages.

## 5. Academic And Support Culture

- The academics page says the school aims for academic excellence while also moulding each learner into a well-balanced being.
- The page emphasizes innovative and enquiring minds.
- The school explicitly highlights values, weekly assembly, parental involvement, and communication.
- A professional support team is called out on the site: remedial educators, an occupational therapist, and a psychologist.
- The school rewards academic, sport, and cultural achievement through an annual award ceremony.

## 7. Hostel And Community Life

- The Margaret Style Hostel page describes the hostel as a home away from home with lots of laughs and love.
- The hostel was founded in 1962 with 29 boarders.
- The hostel page emphasizes independence, time management, hand bells, laundry support, and a warm, structured residential environment.
- The page mentions hostel week, end-of-year formal dress, and rewards for neat rooms and support.

## 8. Fees And Application Requirements

- The applications page states only online applications are accepted.
- The downloadable application checklist includes:
  - Birth certificate / identity document of the learner
  - ID for parent/legal guardian and debtor if not the guardian
  - Passport/study permit/permanent residence permit for non-South African citizens
  - Latest school report
  - Recent photograph of the learner
  - Proof of income/employment/SARS/company registration documents for self-employed parents
  - Copy of medical aid card
  - Health profile / vaccination record
  - Divorce and/or custody court order
  - Proof of residence not older than 3 months
  - Motivational letter to the principal
- The fees page lists annual fees for pre-primary, primary, and the Margaret Style Hostel.

## 9. Visual And Brand Guidance For Eunice Admissions

Use these cues when designing the admissions dashboard and related preview surfaces:

- Deep green as the dominant structural color.
- Warm gold as the accent and selected-state color.
- Cream and soft ivory as the main canvas and card surfaces.
- A disciplined, calm, prestigious feel rather than flashy UI.
- Education-first language that sounds serious, caring, and academically grounded.
- Strong trust cues:
  - long history
  - location in Bloemfontein
  - principal-led contact structure
  - staff visibility
  - support team visibility
  - music/sport/culture breadth
  - hostel and community warmth

## 11. Practical Copy Directions For The Dashboard

Prefer copy that sounds like:

- “academic excellence”
- “well-balanced learner”
- “social responsibility”
- “all-round development”
- “confidence, communication, and teamwork”
- “home away from home”
- “support team”
- “values and tradition”

Avoid copy that sounds like:

- generic SaaS jargon
- startup language
- overly casual admissions tone
- product messaging that ignores the school’s history and culture
